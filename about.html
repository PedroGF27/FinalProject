About

What we intend to do is bring more awareness to penguins

We want to bring awareness to global warming and the effects it has on penguins. We are a charity for penguins. Our plan is to build a bigger community and bring more awareness around this situation. Due to global warming and other human activities, 11 out of the total 18 species of penguin are globally threatened. Donations are much appreciated but even just spreading the word is major help.

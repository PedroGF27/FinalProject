Our charity

Our charity does all it can in order to spread the word and help fight against global warming. We have our own group of researchers and some centers where we take care of penguins. We make sure that the penguins are always healthy, happy, and taken care of. We use all donations to expand these centers to ensure no penguin goes extinct!
